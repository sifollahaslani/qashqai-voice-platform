# Community Governance Notes

## Purpose
These notes outline the early governance direction for QashqAI Voice.

## Governance idea
QashqAI Voice should not operate only as a technical project. It should develop as a culturally responsible initiative with community awareness, ethical oversight, and respect for the social meaning of language.

## Key governance principles
- cultural dignity
- transparency
- accountability
- non-extractive practice
- human oversight
- respect for community context

## Governance questions
- who can approve participation processes?
- how should sensitive material be handled?
- what cultural boundaries should be respected?
- how should disputes or withdrawal requests be managed?
- how should data access be limited or reviewed?

## Early model
At the current stage, governance is founder-led with a responsibility to move toward broader advisory or community-informed structures over time.

## Risks
- over-centralization
- unclear authority boundaries
- weak documentation
- technical activity without enough cultural review

## Direction
The project should evolve toward a documented and reviewable cultural governance model.
