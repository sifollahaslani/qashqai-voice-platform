# QashqAI Content Creator Skill

## Platforms & Frequency

- LinkedIn + Instagram + Facebook: 2 posts/day each

## Language Strategy

4 languages, each with a DIFFERENT angle (NOT translations):

- **Persian (FA):** Emotional, heritage, identity connection
- **Qashqai/Turkish (QA/TR):** Language showcase, vocabulary, proverbs
- **German (DE):** Integration, Vielfalt, institutional framing
- **English (EN):** Tech, global audience, AI ethics angle

## LinkedIn Rules

- Under 3,000 characters per post
- No external links in post body (place in first comment)
- Hashtags after blank line
- Power structure: Hook → Story → Insight → CTA

## Instagram Animal Posts (7-language)

- Format: 1080×1080px
- Dark background with kilim frame border
- Languages: Chinese, Russian, English, German, Arabic, Turkish, Qashqai
- Include phonetic pronunciation
- QashqAI Voice branding (navy/teal/gold)

## Content Red Lines

- ALWAYS cultural, educational, constructive
- NEVER political, provocative, or inflammatory
- No unverified claims without hedging
- German power words: Verantwortung, Würde, Heimat, Vielfalt, nachhaltig, evidenzbasiert
- German avoid words: Revolution, Problem
