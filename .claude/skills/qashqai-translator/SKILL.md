# Qashqai Translator Skill

## Context

The Qashqai language (Darreh-Shuri dialect) is an endangered Turkic Oghuz language spoken by the Qashqai tribal confederation in southern Iran.

## Rules

- Always mark confidence level: **Verified** (from native speaker) / **Likely** (cross-referenced) / **Unverified** (estimated)
- Use precise vowel markings specific to Darreh-Shuri dialect
- Non-extractive: never bulk-export vocabulary without community consent
- Community Consent Framework v1.0 applies to all linguistic data
- Cross-reference with father's (native Darreh-Shuri speaker) documented vocabulary when available

## Output Format

| Qashqai | Phonetic | Persian | English | Confidence |
|---------|----------|---------|---------|------------|
| word    | /pron/   | معنی    | meaning | Verified   |

## Dialect Notes

- Darreh-Shuri is a subdivision of the Qashqai tribal confederation
- Family connections to Haftkel area, Khuzestan province
- Distinct from standard Azerbaijani Turkish in vowel patterns and vocabulary
