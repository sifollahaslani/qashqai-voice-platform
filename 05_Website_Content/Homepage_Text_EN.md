# QashqAI Voice

## Preserving language, dignity, and cultural memory in the age of AI

QashqAI Voice is a cultural-technology initiative dedicated to preserving the Qashqai language through ethical AI, digital inclusion, and community-centered documentation.

Many major languages are increasingly supported by digital tools and artificial intelligence. But low-resource and endangered languages are often left outside this transformation. This is not only a technical gap. It is also a question of visibility, cultural continuity, access, and dignity.

QashqAI Voice is being developed as a responsible foundation for language preservation in the digital age. The project combines consent-based documentation, cultural sensitivity, transparent governance, and future pathways for low-resource language technology.

## Our focus
- endangered language preservation
- ethical and inclusive AI
- community-centered documentation
- cultural dignity and continuity
- responsible digital futures

## Current direction
QashqAI Voice is currently building its early institutional, ethical, and technical foundation for long-term development and collaboration.
