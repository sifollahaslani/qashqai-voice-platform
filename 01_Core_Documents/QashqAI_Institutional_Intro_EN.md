# Institutional Introduction

QashqAI Voice is an emerging cultural-technology initiative focused on preserving the Qashqai language through ethical AI, digital inclusion, and community-centered cultural documentation.

The initiative addresses the growing imbalance between technologically supported major languages and underrepresented low-resource languages that remain excluded from modern digital systems.

QashqAI Voice is being developed as a responsible framework for:
- consent-based language documentation
- culturally aware governance
- transparent and ethical AI use
- long-term support for linguistic and cultural continuity

The project is particularly relevant to institutions working in cultural preservation, endangered languages, inclusive innovation, digital rights, ethical AI, and multilingual access.

QashqAI Voice is currently building its early institutional and technical foundation and is open to dialogue, collaboration, and strategic partnerships.
