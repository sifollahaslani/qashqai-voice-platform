# QashqAI Voice

## A cultural-technology initiative for endangered language preservation, digital inclusion, and ethical AI

QashqAI Voice is a multilingual cultural-technology initiative dedicated to preserving the Qashqai language through ethical, community-centered AI.

The project responds to a structural problem: while major languages are increasingly supported by digital systems and AI tools, low-resource and endangered languages remain underrepresented or completely excluded. This exclusion is not only technical. It affects cultural visibility, intergenerational continuity, educational access, and human dignity.

QashqAI Voice aims to create a responsible foundation for language preservation by combining consent-based voice documentation, community-sensitive governance, and practical pathways toward future low-resource language technologies.

The initiative is grounded in several core principles:
- community consent
- cultural dignity
- transparency
- human oversight
- responsible data governance
- non-extractive technology development

At its current stage, QashqAI Voice is building an initial project structure that includes:
- foundational documentation
- ethical governance logic
- MVP planning
- outreach preparation
- future pathways for speech and language technology support

QashqAI Voice seeks collaboration with institutions, researchers, cultural partners, and funders who work in language preservation, inclusive technology, ethical AI, and cultural rights.
