\# Consent \& Ethics — QashqAI Voice



\## Core Principles

\- Community consent before any data collection

\- Cultural sovereignty over all Qashqai content

\- Non-extractive data policy

\- Transparent AI documentation



\## Data Sources

\- Audio recordings from native Qashqai speakers

\- Family member recordings (primary source)

\- Community-approved vocabulary



\## What Is Forbidden

\- Sharing raw audio without consent

\- Commercial use of community data

\- Publishing sacred or private knowledge

\- Training other AI models without permission
