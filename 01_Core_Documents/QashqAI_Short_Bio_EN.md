# QashqAI Voice – Short Bio

QashqAI Voice is a cultural-technology initiative founded by Siefollah Aslani to support the preservation of the Qashqai language through ethical AI, digital inclusion, and community-centered cultural documentation.

The project focuses on building responsible foundations for low-resource language support by combining language preservation, consent-based documentation, cultural dignity, and transparent technology governance.

QashqAI Voice is designed not only as a documentation effort, but as a long-term contribution to a more inclusive digital future in which underrepresented languages and communities are not left behind.
