# Immigration (Ausländerbehörde / BAMF / Aufenthalt / Duldung) — Germany

> General information and signposting only. Not legal advice. Immigration
> deadlines are short and consequences can be irreversible — verify with a
> qualified advisor urgently.

## In short (VERIFIED, general)
- Most decisions of the Ausländerbehörde or BAMF can be challenged, but only
  within a **strict time limit**.
- Specialised migration counselling exists and is often free.

## Check on your document (VERIFY)
- The **date received** (deadlines often run from delivery — keep the envelope).
- The type of decision (rejection, time limit, removal notice) and its
  Rechtsbehelfsbelehrung.

## Competent institutions (types)
- **Ausländerbehörde** (issuing office) / **BAMF** (asylum matters).
- **Migrationsberatung (MBE)** / **Jugendmigrationsdienst (JMD)**.
- **Flüchtlingsrat** / **Refugee Law Clinic** (local).
- **Fachanwalt für Migrationsrecht** (specialised lawyer).

## Evidence to collect
- The full letter + envelope, passport and residence documents
  (Aufenthaltstitel / Duldung), and your prior file/correspondence.

## When to get human help immediately
- Any mention of Abschiebung (removal), a removal date, or a near deadline →
  contact a specialised advisor or lawyer **immediately**.
