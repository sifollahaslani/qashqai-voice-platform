# Disability (Schwerbehinderung / Pflegegrad / Widerspruch) — Germany

> General information and signposting only. Not legal advice.

## In short (VERIFIED, general)
- A rejected or too-low assessment — degree of disability (**GdB**) or care
  level (**Pflegegrad**) — can usually be challenged with a **Widerspruch**.
- Free, independent disability advice is available (EUTB).

## Check on your document (VERIFY)
- The Bescheid **date** and its Rechtsbehelfsbelehrung.
- The assessed value vs. what your medical situation supports.

## Competent institutions (types)
- **Versorgungsamt / zuständige Behörde** (GdB) and **Pflegekasse** (Pflegegrad).
- **EUTB** — Ergänzende unabhängige Teilhabeberatung (free, independent).
- **Sozialverband** (e.g. VdK / SoVD) — membership-based support.

## Evidence to collect
- The full decision letter (with date) and medical reports / Befunde supporting
  your condition; any prior assessments.

## Templates
- `objection_letter_de.md`, `evidence_checklist_de.md`

## When to get human help
- Before the objection deadline passes, ask EUTB or a Sozialverband to review
  the Bescheid with you.
