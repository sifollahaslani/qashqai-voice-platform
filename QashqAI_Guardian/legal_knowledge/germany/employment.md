# Employment (Arbeit / Kündigung / Lohn / Vertrag) — Germany

> General information and signposting only. Not legal advice. Employment
> deadlines can be **very short** — verify urgently.

## In short (VERIFIED, general)
- A dismissal-protection claim (**Kündigungsschutzklage**) has a notably short
  deadline. Acting late can forfeit the claim — verify the exact period fast.
- Unpaid wages can usually be claimed; document the amounts carefully.

## Check on your document (VERIFY)
- The **date you received** the Kündigung and any stated deadline.
- The contract terms (notice period, working hours, pay).

## Competent institutions (types)
- **Betriebsrat** (works council) and **Gewerkschaft** (union, for members).
- **Arbeitsagentur** (register early to protect benefits).
- **Arbeitsgericht** (labour court) — note the short dismissal-claim deadline.
- **Fachanwalt für Arbeitsrecht** (specialised lawyer).

## Evidence to collect
- The Arbeitsvertrag, the Kündigung (with date received), payslips
  (Lohnabrechnungen), proof of unpaid amounts, and all written contact.
