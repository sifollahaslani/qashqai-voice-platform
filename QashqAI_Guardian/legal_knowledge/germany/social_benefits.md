# Social benefits (Jobcenter / Bürgergeld / Sozialamt / Wohngeld) — Germany

> General information and signposting only. Not legal advice.

## In short (VERIFIED, general)
- A benefit decision (Bescheid) can usually be challenged with a formal
  objection (**Widerspruch**) within a stated deadline.
- **Bürgergeld** is administered by the **Jobcenter**;
  **Grundsicherung / Sozialhilfe** by the **Sozialamt**;
  **Wohngeld** by the **Wohngeldstelle**.

## Check on your document (VERIFY)
- The Bescheid **date** and its Rechtsbehelfsbelehrung (where/when to object).
- What exactly was reduced, refused, or reclaimed, and the reason given.

## Competent institutions (types)
- **Jobcenter**, **Sozialamt**, **Wohngeldstelle**.
- Independent **Sozialberatung** (welfare counselling) — free help reading the
  Bescheid and preparing a Widerspruch.

## Evidence to collect
- The full Bescheid (with date), proof of income/rent/household, and your
  original application and any earlier decisions.

## Templates

## When to get human help
- If benefits stop entirely or money is reclaimed → seek Sozialberatung quickly;
  there is usually a short objection deadline.
