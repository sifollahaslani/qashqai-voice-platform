## Who We Are

**QashqAI Voice** is the first human-governed cultural AI infrastructure designed to preserve endangered languages with community consent, cultural data sovereignty, and operational trust.

We begin with the **Darreh-Shuri Qashqai language** — a critically endangered Turkic/Oghuz dialect of southern Iran. But our mission is global: to build a living, replicable model that any community can adopt to protect their language, dignity, and cultural memory.

---

## The Problem

Every two weeks, a language dies. With it disappears an entire universe of knowledge, identity, and human heritage.

Most preservation efforts **archive loss** — they document what is disappearing, but they do not build systems for survival.

Meanwhile, the rise of AI creates a new threat: **extractive data collection** that treats cultural and linguistic material as raw training data — without consent, without sovereignty, without accountability.

**There is no global infrastructure that combines language preservation with ethical AI governance.**

Until now.

---

## Our Answer

**QashqAI Voice** builds **living, governable, culturally legitimate AI infrastructure for language survival.**

We do not simply collect data. We design the ethical and technical architecture that determines:

- **Who** may use cultural data
- **Under what conditions**
- **With what accountability**
- **For whose benefit**
