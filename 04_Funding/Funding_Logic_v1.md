# Funding Logic v1

## Purpose
This document explains why QashqAI Voice requires early-stage support and how funding would create meaningful institutional, cultural, and technical value.

## Core funding rationale
QashqAI Voice addresses a structural gap: endangered and low-resource languages are often excluded from digital systems, AI tools, and future-facing infrastructure.

This exclusion has consequences beyond language loss. It affects:
- cultural continuity
- digital inclusion
- educational access
- representation in technology
- long-term community visibility

## Why funding is needed now
Early support is necessary to build a credible foundation before large-scale technical development becomes possible.

At this stage, funding would support:
- project documentation
- governance and consent frameworks
- outreach and institutional preparation
- initial data logic and documentation planning
- MVP structuring
- early coordination capacity

## Why this project is fundable
QashqAI Voice sits at the intersection of several important priorities:
- endangered language preservation
- ethical AI
- inclusive innovation
- cultural rights
- community-centered documentation
- digital justice

## Funding case
The project is not asking for support for a vague idea. It is building a structured early framework that can later support documentation, partnerships, research, and practical low-resource language technology pathways.

## Strategic value for funders
Supporting QashqAI Voice allows a funder or institution to contribute to:
- cultural preservation in the digital age
- ethical AI for underrepresented communities
- responsible data practices
- visibility for marginalized linguistic communities
- an early model for non-extractive cultural technology work

## Current funding stage
Early-stage / seed-stage support is the most relevant category at the current moment.
