# Donor Q&A v1

## What is QashqAI Voice?
QashqAI Voice is a cultural-technology initiative focused on preserving the Qashqai language through ethical AI, digital inclusion, and community-centered documentation.

## Why is this project important?
Many endangered and low-resource languages remain excluded from digital systems and AI tools. Without intentional work, these communities risk further marginalization in the digital future.

## Why focus on Qashqai?
The Qashqai language represents a meaningful case of cultural and linguistic value that is underrepresented in technological systems. Supporting it is both a preservation effort and a digital justice issue.

## Is this only a language documentation project?
No. It is also a governance, inclusion, and ethical technology project. The aim is to create a responsible foundation for future low-resource language support.

## What stage is the project in?
The project is in an early structuring phase. It is currently building its institutional, ethical, and technical foundations.

## What would funding support?
Funding would support:
- core documentation
- outreach preparation
- governance frameworks
- consent structure
- technical planning
- early data logic

## What makes this project credible?
Its credibility comes from combining:
- a clear cultural mission
- ethical safeguards
- structured documentation
- practical MVP thinking
- a long-term institutional direction

## What are the main risks?
Main risks include:
- limited early resources
- capacity constraints
- slow institutional engagement
- technical complexity over time

## How are these risks being managed?
The current strategy is to begin with a lean, document-driven, governance-first foundation before attempting scale.

## What kind of support is most useful now?
The most useful support now is:
- seed funding
- strategic guidance
- institutional dialogue
- research collaboration
- early credibility-building partnerships
