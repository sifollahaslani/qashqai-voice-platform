## 4. The QashqAI Protocol (QCPP)

**QashqAI Cultural-AI Preservation Protocol**

A proposed global standard for ethical endangered language preservation with AI:

### 4.1 — Individual & Collective Consent
Every speaker gives informed consent. Communities have collective decision-making authority over how their language data is used.

### 4.2 — Right to Data Withdrawal
Any speaker can withdraw their data at any time. Withdrawal is immediate, complete, and irreversible from all systems.

### 4.3 — Cultural Sovereignty Over Voice & Text
The community — not the organization, not the AI model — holds sovereign authority over all linguistic and cultural data.

### 4.4 — Prohibition of Colonial Data Extraction
No data may be extracted, sold, sublicensed, or used for purposes not explicitly approved by the community.

### 4.5 — Responsible AI Use
All AI systems used in the pipeline must be transparent, auditable, and subject to human oversight at every stage.

### 4.6 — Human Oversight at All Stages
No fully automated decision-making on cultural data. Every critical process includes human review.

### 4.7 — Ethical & Financial Compensation Model
Speakers and communities are not "sources" — they are partners. Fair compensation (financial, ethical, or communal) is a requirement.

## 5. The QashqAI Benchmark

**QashqAI Endangered Language Trust Benchmark**

The world's first evaluation framework designed specifically for endangered languages:

| Dimension | What We Measure |
|---|---|
| **Low-Resource Accuracy** | ASR/TTS performance on languages with minimal training data |
| **Elder Speech Recognition** | Accuracy on elderly speakers — the primary carriers of endangered languages |
| **Dialectal Variation** | Handling of intra-language diversity (not just "standard" forms) |
| **Consent & Legitimacy Quality** | Whether the data pipeline meets ethical and cultural consent standards |
| **AI Output Trustworthiness** | Confidence levels, hallucination rates, and cultural appropriateness of outputs |

We don't just build models. **We define what success means** for endangered language AI.
